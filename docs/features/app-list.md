# Corely Apps & Templates (Priority Order)

This list ranks **Apps (modules/bounded contexts)** and **Templates (configuration/presets)** from **most important → less important** for an SMB ERP baseline. It’s designed to help you build a country-neutral core first, then add deeper vertical capabilities later.

---

## Apps (Modules / Bounded Contexts)

### Tier 0 — Platform foundation (required for everything)

1. **Tenant / Workspace / Settings**
2. **Identity & Access (RBAC)**
3. **Party** (unified Customer / Supplier / Employee / Contact)
4. **Documents & Attachments** (files everywhere)
5. **Audit Log + Activity Timeline**
6. **Workflow / Approvals (Cross-module)**
7. **AI Copilot Platform** (tool cards + apply actions + provenance logging)

---

### Tier 1 — Financial spine (core SMB baseline)

8. **Accounting (GL + periods + reports)**
9. **Sales Billing (Invoices + Credit Notes + AR)**
10. **Purchasing / AP (Vendor Bills + Aging)**
11. **Bank Import + Reconciliation**
12. **Expenses (Employee expenses → approvals → AP/accounting)**

---

### Tier 2 — Revenue engine (growth)

13. **CRM (Leads/Deals/Activities)**
14. **Sales (Quotes → Orders → Invoices)**
15. **POS (Android-first client)**

---

### Tier 3 — Operations engine (true ERP capabilities)

16. **Inventory (Products, warehouses, stock moves, availability)**
17. **Procurement (POs, receiving; 3-way match later)**
18. **Fulfillment / Shipping (pick/pack/ship, delivery notes)**

---

### Tier 4 — Service-business “second spine” (high ROI for many SMBs)

19. **Projects**
20. **Time Tracking / Timesheets**
21. **Scheduling & Booking (engine)**

---

### Tier 5 — Engagement & retention layer (strong for repeat-visit SMBs)

22. **Engagement (Check-in + Loyalty + later Feedback/Campaigns)**

---

### Tier 6 — People ops (often becomes country-specific)

23. **HR basics** (employee profiles, time-off)
24. **Payroll** (usually localization-heavy → later / partner integrations)

---

### Tier 7 — Advanced / vertical ops (powerful, but heavy)

25. **Manufacturing / MRP**
26. **Warehouse Management (WMS) advanced**
27. **Asset Management / Fixed Assets**
28. **Helpdesk / Customer Support**
29. **E-commerce connectors**
30. **BI / Planning (budgets, forecasting, KPI studio)**

---

## Templates (Configuration / Presets)

### Tier A — Finance templates (highest leverage)

1. **Chart of Accounts templates** (by country + SMB type)
2. **Tax/VAT profile templates + posting mappings**
3. **Document numbering templates** (invoices, bills, receipts, POS)
4. **Document layouts** (invoice/quote/PO PDF + email templates)

---

### Tier B — Sales/CRM templates (fast activation)

5. **CRM pipeline templates** (stages, win/loss reasons)
6. **Sales defaults templates** (payment terms, dunning cadence, quote policies)
7. **Customer/Supplier segmentation templates** (tags, risk flags, categories)

---

### Tier C — Inventory & procurement templates (operational defaults)

8. **Warehouse/location skeleton templates** (receiving/stock/shipping)
9. **UoM sets + product category templates**
10. **Reorder policy presets** (min/max, reorder point)
11. **Procurement policy templates** (PO lifecycle, receiving policy, approval thresholds)

---

### Tier D — POS templates (Android-first)

12. **POS layout templates** (retail quick-sell vs service checkout)
13. **Receipt templates** (branding, footer, legal lines)
14. **Payment method templates** (cash/card/external terminal flows)

---

### Tier E — Scheduling templates (engine-based)

15. **Service Booking template** (customer required, reminders, staff assignment)
16. **Manufacturing Operation template** (machines/workcenters, setup buffers)
17. **Maintenance template** (downtime blocks, recurring reservations)

---

### Tier F — Engagement templates (check-in + loyalty)

18. **Check-in flow templates** (self-service kiosk vs staff-assisted, required fields)
19. **Loyalty program templates** (points-per-visit, tiers later, reward catalogs)

---

### Tier G — Vertical packs (bundle of apps + templates)

20. **Retail pack** (POS + Inventory + Purchasing + Accounting defaults)
21. **Service business pack** (Scheduling + POS + Engagement + CRM defaults)
22. **DTC / e-commerce pack** (Inventory + shipping + connectors)
23. **Light manufacturing pack** (Inventory + Purchasing + MRP templates)

---
